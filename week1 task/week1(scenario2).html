Filter coffee

Firstly, filter coffee powder has a richer flavor and aroma than instant coffee powder. Secondly, filter coffee is made with fresh ingredients, while instant coffee is made with pre-ground, roasted coffee. Thirdly, filter coffee is brewed using hot water, while instant coffee is brewed with cold water.

Croissant

A Croissant is typically made of yeast-rien dough. The doughis first layered with butter and then rolled. It is folded many times in a process called lamination. then the dough is cut into lamination. Then the dough is cut into triangles, rolled to form a cresent shape and baked. The lamination process gives croissants its flaky texture and fluffy shaped.
